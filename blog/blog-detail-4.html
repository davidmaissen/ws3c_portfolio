Digital guide for outdoor photography

Tags: E-Books Photography
Outdoor photography has many facets, be it untouched nature, architecture or the creative play of light and shadow. It is precisely this diversity that makes capturing these special moments a challenge. The free e-book from Ifolor teaches the principles of outdoor photography in the form of equipment tips and motif ideas, as well as the basics of image composition. Anyone who likes to get out and about in nature to find interesting motifs can also be inspired by the suggestions for hiking routes in Germany and Switzerland.
