Capture and share ideas!

Tags: Board Design
Moodboards capture initial ideas, map color and font concepts, and thus support the entire design process. For customers, mood boards can also be the basis for presentations and unite the opinions and ideas of all members in teams. Figma as a design tool is known for its multiplayer editing. So why not simply create the digital mood boards in Figma in the future and enrich them with colors and images? Once all the ideas are collected or the first concept ideas are born, you can easily share the board with your team or client. Comments can be entered and answered directly on the board.
